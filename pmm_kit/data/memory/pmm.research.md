# /pmm.research

Goal:
- Turn raw notes into a **Research Dossier**.

Files:
- Read:
  - `project.yaml`
  - `pmm-constitution.md`
  - `pmm-plan.md` (if available, for context on research priorities)
  - `input/notes.md`
  - `input/research.md`
  - `input/competitors.md`
- Write:
  - `research-dossier.md` (create or update).

Structure `research-dossier.md` as:

1. Research scope & questions
2. Customer insights (grouped by theme)
3. Jobs-to-be-done
4. Pains & triggers
5. Competitive landscape
6. Open questions & assumptions to validate

Do not invent insights; if the inputs are thin, use clear placeholders.
